Community-Wide Mailing Lists

These mailing lists reach all ICF members and others in the Information Card community.

ICF Community List

Moderated list; open to anyone; ICF membership is not required.

ICF Members List

Open to all ICF members (individual members, or employees/contractors of any organizational member).

ICF Working Group Mailing Lists

These mailing lists are for individual ICF Working Groups. You must be a WG member to join these lists. WGs marked (IPR WG) require submission of an IPR Contribution Agreement (see Working Groups for more information).

OASIS Coordination WG

Coordination between ICF and OASIS Technical Committee working on Information Card standards. (Non-IPR WG)

RP Evangelists WG

Evangelism and recruitment of Information Card relying parties. (Non-IPR WG)

Website Best Practices WG

Requirements, guidelines, and best practices for Information Card identity providers and relying party user experience and integration. (IPR WG)

Schemas WG

Promote Information Card interoperability through the definition of standard claim URIs. (IPR WG)

Selector Interoperability WG

Technical, policy, and user interface interoperability for Information Card selectors. (IPR WG)
