It is perhaps best-known for its semi-annual conference, <a href="http://www.internetidentityworkshop.com/">Internet Identity Workshop</a> (now known as “IIW”), which brings together a wide range of people active in the Internet identity community to forge the protocols, policies, and partnerships necessary to produce an identity layer serving all constituencies: people, for-profit companies, non-profit organizations, and governments.</p> <p>The current Identity Commons is actually the second generation of an organization originally founded by Owen Davis and Andrew Nelson in 2002.
