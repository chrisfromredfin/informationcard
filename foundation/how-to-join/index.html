Membership Application Process

To join the Information Card Foundation, please send us your membership application by following these instructions:

  1. To join as an organization, download this form. To join as an individual, download this form.
  2. Return two signed copies to ICF's mailing address at:
    Information Card Foundation
    ATTN: Dan Perron
    56 Kearney Road
    Needham, MA 02494
    -- OR --
    Fax a copy to: 309 424 1075
    -- OR --
    Email signed .jpg, .png, .eps, or .psd files to
    director ---at--- informationcard.net
  3. Dues may be paid by check, ETF, PO/invoice, or Paypal.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
