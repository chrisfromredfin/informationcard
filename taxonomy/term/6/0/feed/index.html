"Cookies solved the problem of how a user could share information across the pages of the same website. For example, without them, e-commerce shopping carts would not work. Information Cards solve the next wider problem: how a user can safely share private information across completely <em>different</em> websites of their choosing."</p> <p>The paper uses the common example of booking travel. Today, a traveller must re-enter their trip details into every travel site they visit. Even though that travel site may set a cookie to recognize the returning user, that's all the cookie can "remember".
